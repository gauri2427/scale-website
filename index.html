Company

Social Connect Agri Livelihood Enterprise Private Limited

The idea for a fresh milk and milk products delivery business to the local community in Latur, Maharashtra, was born out of a desire to provide high-quality, locally sourced buffalo milk and milk products directly to consumers' homes. To fulfill this purpose, we conducted market research. We traced contact numbers from friends and relatives, and also hired people to conduct surveys. We briefed customers about our products and the motto behind our business. Customers favored and supported our business concept, which involved delivering fresh milk directly from the farm to homes without any adulteration or processing. This approach also aimed to support local farmers. Based on the positive responses collected from customers, we developed a robust business plan for a fresh buffalo milk and milk products home delivery service, which commenced on June 1, 2020.

Vision

To be leading and most trusted fresh buffalo milk and milk products delivery service by enhancing the health and lives of customers by providing them farm fresh milk and milk products with the highest quality delivered directly to their homes, ensuring convenience, sustainability and every household can enjoy the goodness of fresh milk and milk prodcuts delivered right to their doorstep.

Mission

Our mission is to enhance the daily lives of families and individuals by providing convenient, reliable and sustainable farm-fresh, high-quality milk ad milk products, delivered with utmost care and efficiency. We are also committed to supporting local dairy farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture practices, and fostering healthy communities through our home delivery service.

Why Choose Us?

At SCALE, we take immense pride in offering unparalleled quality and purity in our milk and milk products. We prioritize the well-being of our dairy animals, ensuring they receive the best care, nutrition, and living conditions. The result is milk of exceptional quality, rich in nutrients and flavor.
